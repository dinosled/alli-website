Alli

Made to support people during the process of abortion with pills.

Alli-App

Alli-App offers daily information and guidance on taking medication, managing potential side effects, and ensuring a safe and comfortable experience during the process of abortion with pills. We understand the importance of having a low-barrier, diversity-aware, and gender-inclusive platform that serves the needs of our diverse target audience. Our goal is to provide a comprehensive and inclusive app that supports individuals through every step of the process.

About

At Alli, we are a dedicated team of two doctors, a programmer, and a designer, working towards revolutionizing the way people experience abortion with pills. We are currently in the development stage. If you want to join our mission to make abortion with pills a more accessible and empowering experience reach out to us.
